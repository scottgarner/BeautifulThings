About

Making Things Beautiful / Making Beautiful Things is an interactive book that investigates the difficulties and delights associated with the human drive to create. I wrote it for my master's thesis to explore my creative process and perhaps help others struggling with their own.

Early Access

This book is available as an "early access" product while I continue to expand and refine the content and functionality. Updates will be delivered automatically and at no cost. If you stumble across any problems or errors, please file an issue.

Purchase

Making Things Beautiful / Making Beautiful Things was built exclusively for Apple iBooks on your iPad or Mac. You can buy it or download the first chapter for free from the iBooks Store.
